Principal Investigator

Dr. Di Wu

Assistant Professor, ERAU AE | Director, XDLab

Di Wu received his PhD from UC San Diego advised by Aaron Rosengren and worked as a Postdoc in MIT advised by Richard Linares. His research is positioned at the intersection of the rapidly proliferating field of space engineering, the continuous growth of machine learning and artificial intelligence, and the solid foundations in computation, numerical methods, optimization, dynamics, and control, with the application in learning and autonomy in orbit environment and space systems.

XDLab aims at promoting deep integration of space engineering and artificial intelligence and pushing limit on space engineering methodology innovation for efficient and sustainable anthropogenic space development.
